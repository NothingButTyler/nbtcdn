# MCH (Mickey's Crazy Horrors)
Mickey's Crazy Horrors is a comedy skit series I've made using voiceovers of Mickey, Minnie, Donald, Daisy, and Goofy. I've also made other characters in the series as well such as Bill Johnson and Copsillean Mouse in the Mickey Refuses to do The Dishes, which was a special series with multiple parts combined together as a movie. It was a fun time to make these!

All of the original series are stored here in the mch folder. Specials Links are in the following folders below.
