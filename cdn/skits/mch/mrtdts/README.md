# mrtdts (Mickey Refuses To Do The Dishes)
Yeah...there was a typo. mrtdts isn't supposed to have an S at the end, it's supposed to be the letter D. I finally noticed that. Anyways, 

Mickey Refuses To Do The Dishes is a special movie skit where Minnie asks Mickey to do the dishes and he refuses because Minnie gets to have short breaks and he doesn't. Lots of crazy stuff happens that I can barely describe myself, such as Mickey leaving the clubhouse, new characters, a BATTLE SCENE?!?! And so much more plot twists that you won't see coming!
